What is E-Medicare?

E-Medicare is a consumer healthcare “super app”.

E-Medicare provides consumers with on-demand, home-delivered access to a wide range of prescription and OTC pharmaceutical products, other consumer healthcare products, comprehensive diagnostic test services, and teleconsultations, thereby serving their healthcare needs.

Our branches are spreading across all over India.
